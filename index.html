What Is TipEASE?

TipEASE is a company dedicated in making tipping service workers easy.
TipEASE aims to solve the issue of service workers having to share tips with their employers
or not recieving their tips all together. This issue alone gives customers the drive to use TipEASE!

Why Use TipEASE?

  • Supports Service Workers

    TipEASE gives you the ability to tip service providers directly, making tips available quickly with no employer issues.

  • Quick And Effortless

    TipEASE makes tipping quick and easy. No more dealing with cash and cards.

  • Tip Later

    TipEASE allows you to tip at a later time if you were in a hurry, or simply forgot.

  • Boundless Uses

    TipEASE gives you the ability to tip different types of service providers. Hair Stylists, Waitresses, and even Tutors!

    With TipEASE you can track how much, how often, and where you tip.
